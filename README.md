# 🌿 GreenTrace – Indian College Student Carbon Footprint Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

GreenTrace is an environmentally focused application designed to help Indian college students measure, monitor, and reduce their carbon footprint. The platform calculates environmental impact based on daily activities such as transportation, electricity consumption, and lifestyle habits.

The project promotes environmental awareness and sustainable living through data-driven insights and personalized recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Raise environmental awareness among students.
- Track daily carbon emissions.
- Encourage sustainable lifestyle choices.
- Provide actionable environmental insights.

---

## ✨ Features

✅ Carbon Footprint Calculator

✅ Transportation Emission Tracking

✅ Energy Consumption Monitoring

✅ Sustainability Dashboard

✅ Personalized Recommendations

✅ Environmental Awareness Reports
